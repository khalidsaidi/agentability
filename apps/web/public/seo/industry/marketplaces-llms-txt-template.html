FAQ

Does llms.txt directly improve rankings?

It improves machine comprehension and routing to your canonical docs. Combined with crawlable content and strong internal links, it contributes to discoverability and traffic quality.

What should be in llms.txt first?

Start with product URL, API endpoint, OpenAPI link, docs index, contact info, and verification resources.
