# Agentability Privacy

Last updated: 2026-02-11

## Data we process
We process the URL you submit and the public resources we fetch to generate a report.

## How we use data
We use fetched public data to compute evaluation results and evidence bundles.

## Storage
Reports and evidence may be stored to enable sharing and verification.

## Cookies and analytics
Essential storage is used for core product behavior (for example, cookie-consent preference and onboarding state).
Optional analytics cookies are only enabled after explicit consent and can be rejected or changed from cookie settings.
See `/legal/cookies.md` for details.
